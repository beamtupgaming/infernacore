YOUR PRIVACY — A PLAIN-LANGUAGE SUMMARY
========================================

InfernaCore is built on a single principle: your machine, your data.

  NO network connections       — the app never contacts the internet.
  NO telemetry or analytics    — nothing about your usage is recorded.
  NO background processes      — nothing runs when the app is closed.
  NO hidden registry changes   — only a standard HKCU uninstall entry is written.
  NO third-party dependencies  — no cloud SDKs, no tracking libraries.

The only files written to your system are:

  • The application files placed in the folder you choose during installation.
  • A config.json in that same folder storing only your game path preference.
  • A standard Windows uninstall entry (removable via Control Panel / Settings).

That's it.  Uninstalling removes all of the above completely.
